## What Is Dependency Theory?

Dependency theory explains persistent poverty in the Global South not through internal deficiencies but through international economic structures designed to transfer wealth to wealthy nations. It challenges modernisation narratives claiming all countries follow the same development path.

## Key Mechanisms

- Commodity export dependence on volatile global prices
- Import of manufactured goods at higher prices
- Capital flight and debt servicing
- Technology monopolies controlling production methods
- Trade rules favouring industrial economies

## Modern Manifestations

- Structural adjustment programs imposing austerity
- Land grabs by foreign investors
- Tax havens enabling profit extraction
- Patent regimes preventing technology transfer
